# Life Manager

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
Personal fitness and lifestyle tracking mobile-first PWA. Training logs, nutrition tracking, weight monitoring, routines, and AI trainer chat.

## Users
Solo user (Adrian). Mobile-first on iPhone. Used daily for workout tracking, food logging, and habit management.

## Brand & Tone
Dark, minimal, high-contrast. Functional over decorative. German UI copy. No playfulness, no corporate. Direct and dense.

## Anti-References
- Overly colorful health apps (MyFitnessPal rainbow UI)
- Gamified fitness apps (badges, streaks, confetti)
- SaaS dashboard templates

## Strategic Principles
1. Speed over decoration: every tap counts during a workout
2. Information density: show data, not chrome
3. Consistency: same patterns everywhere
4. Dark mode native: designed for dark, not adapted from light
